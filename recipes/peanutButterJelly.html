Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description

A recipe for everyone's childhood favorite: The Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of your favorite bread
  • 1 jar of peanut butter of choice
  • 1 jar of jelly, I usually go for grape

Steps

  1. Get out a plate and lay your two slice of bread on it.
  2. With a butter knife, put a quality amount of peanut butter on the left slice.
  3. Again with the butter knife put some jelly on the right slice.
  4. Put the pieces of bread together, making sure the peanut butter and jelly are meeting in the middle.
  5. Enjoy!
