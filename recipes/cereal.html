High Quality Bowl of Cereal

Description

It's Saturday morning and cartoons are on! What are you eating?

Ingredients

  • Favorite Cereal, I like Fruity Pebbles
  • Your milk of choice!

Steps

  1. Grab a bowl and a spoon.
  2. Fill the bowl and 3/4 of the way up with that cereal.
  3. Pour milk until you are satisfied. Be careful not to spill!
  4. Turn on some toons and enjoy!
